Frequently Asked Questions

Can you sign up as a team?

Yes, you can sign up as a team with as many members as you can get (Max 7). If you need subs or more team members, place N/A within the reamining slots on the registration form.

How many teams will be made up of solo players?

We will be creating six randomized teams of solo players. (subject to change)

If I rank up to Gold 3 sometime between now and the tournament, will I still be able to participate?

Yes, players who rank up to Gold 3 may still be able to participate, but must contact tournament organizers first. Players who rank up to Platinum 1 and above will no longer be eligible to participate.

Which regions are allowed to participate?

We are allowing participating players from North America (NA). Viewers are welcome from all regions.

How are servers chosen?

Teams may discuss what server the match will be hosted on. If no server is agreed upon, it wil be decided by the tournament admins.
